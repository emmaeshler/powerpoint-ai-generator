Generate a strategic priorities slide with an integrated horizontal timeline and connected milestone cards.

OBJECTIVE:
The slide must feel executive, directional, and forward-moving — not static columns.

STRUCTURE:

1) TITLE SECTION
- Large, confident headline (bold, high contrast)
- Thin accent rule beneath title (not full width)
- Generous spacing before timeline begins

2) TIMELINE (Upper Third of Slide)
- Horizontal line spanning across slide
- Three evenly spaced circular milestone nodes on the line
- Each node contains a simple, clean icon (no detailed illustrations)
- Quarter label beneath each node (e.g., Q2 2026, Q3 2026, Q4 2026)
- Nodes color-coded:
  - Blue (Operational)
  - Orange (Digital)
  - Green (Talent)
- Subtle drop shadow for nodes
- Clean, minimal aesthetic (no heavy gradients)

3) CONNECTOR LINES
- Thin vertical line extending down from each milestone node
- Small colored anchor dot at end of vertical connector
- Connectors must clearly link timeline to content cards below

4) PRIORITY CARDS (Bottom Half)
- Three cards aligned under respective milestone nodes
- Cards must shrink-wrap to content height (do NOT extend to bottom of slide)
- White card background
- Subtle shadow for depth (very light)

CARD DESIGN:
- Solid colored header bar (matching timeline node color)
- Priority title inside header (left aligned)
- Small quarter pill tag in header (right aligned)
- White content area below header
- 24–32px internal padding
- Maximum 3–4 bullets
- Bullet dots match header color
- Short, executive-style phrasing (no long sentences)

VISUAL WEIGHT:
- Center priority slightly emphasized (either:
  - Slightly larger node
  - Slightly wider card
  - Slightly stronger header tone)
- Emphasis must be subtle, not dramatic

LAYOUT RULES:
- Leave intentional white space below cards (minimum 120px above footer)
- At least 40% of slide should remain neutral/white space
- Avoid full-height colored backgrounds
- No heavy pastel fills
- Maintain clean consulting-grade aesthetic

FOOTER:
- Minimal divider line
- Logo left
- Confidentiality right

TONE:
Executive, restrained, consulting-quality.
Directional and strategic — not decorative.
